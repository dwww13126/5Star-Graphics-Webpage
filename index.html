About Us:

5Star Graphics have specialised in being a print product manufacturer with over 14 years experience across a variety of different print operations. We have worked together to satisfy the needs of many clients throughout the Waikato region.

We utilise a top of the line Roland TrueVIS solvent printer/cutter to print eye-popping colours using long lasting inks. Our machine can print on a variety of different media so we can find creative solutions for your print media needs.

Get in touch to discuss your next print project for personal service and quality results at a realistic price!
